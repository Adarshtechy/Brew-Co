Our Story

Crafting exceptional coffee experiences since 2010

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010 by coffee enthusiast Marco Brewster, Brew & Co. started as a small cart in the heart of the city. With a passion for perfecting the art of coffee making, Marco traveled the world learning from the best baristas and coffee farmers.

Today, we continue that journey of discovery, bringing you the finest single-origin beans and innovative brewing techniques in a warm, welcoming environment.
